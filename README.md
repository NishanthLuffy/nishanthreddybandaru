## About me

I'm a software engineer with 5+ years building high-throughput, event-driven systems that operate at real scale. At Walmart, I own core microservices in a PO creation pipeline that processes **150,000–200,000 purchase orders daily** — from Kafka-based enrichment and validation through to persistence and fulfillment channel distribution.

I care deeply about system design: pluggable patterns, fault-tolerant pipelines, and building things that don't fall over when volume spikes. I'm equally comfortable in the backend (Java, Spring Boot, Kafka) and the frontend (React, Redux, GraphQL), and I've shipped full self-service portals that cut vendor onboarding from 3 days to 4 hours.

Most recently, I designed a **SWF Threshold event** in an event-sourced PO lifecycle architecture that saved **$1B over 2 quarters** across 10 million purchase orders.
